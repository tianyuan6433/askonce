# Relationship Between Pivot and Pivot⁺


#### Q: What is Pivot⁺? What is its relationship with the existing Pivot?

A: MAXHUB Pivot⁺ is a next-generation centralized control product fully upgraded based on the core capabilities of the existing Pivot. Tailored for IT administrators, it supports more scenarios, offers a smoother UI experience, and delivers higher O&M efficiency—perfect for meeting the needs of enterprise-scale management.
MAXHUB Pivot⁺ is a cloud-based CMS + DMS platform designed to centrally manage both devices and content across MAXHUB meeting room and display solutions.It enables organisations to remotely monitor and manage devices while also creating, scheduling, and publishing content to commercial displays and meeting room screens.
Pivot⁺ provides a unified control layer for large-scale deployments, helping IT and operations teams standardise device behaviour, control messaging, and reduce operational complexity across locations.
Kindly note that Pivot⁺ adopts an entirely new technical underlying architecture, which is different from the original Pivot. Therefore, existing organizations cannot migrate to the new Pivot⁺ seamlessly, and simple configuration adjustments are required during migration.

#### Q: Will Pivot be replaced or discontinued by Pivot⁺ in the future?

A: Please rest assured. Pivot's services will continue to run stably throughout 2026 without affecting your normal use, but no new features will be added in subsequent updates.
Starting from July 1, 2026, existing Pivot users can freely choose whether to upgrade to Pivot⁺ to enjoy new services.
We will continue to monitor the migration progress and feedback of existing Pivot users, and then determine the specific shutdown time of Pivot based on actual circumstances, ensuring you have sufficient time to prepare.

#### Q: Why did MAXHUB launch Pivot⁺? What limitations exist in the existing Pivot?

A: With the deepening of enterprise digital transformation, the existing Pivot has gradually exposed bottlenecks that are difficult to meet current needs: the UI interaction and operation process are not quite in line with modern users' aesthetic and efficiency requirements; the compatibility and management of new devices (such as Microsoft MTR series) are complicated; the underlying architecture lacks flexibility, leading to slow feature iterations that cannot keep up with business development.
Pivot⁺ is designed to solve these pain points! Through three core upgrades—reconstructed underlying architecture, optimized interactive experience, and expanded multi-scenario compatibility—it creates a dedicated platform more suitable for enterprise-scale management. It not only makes device management easier and more efficient but also further strengthens MAXHUB's competitive advantage in the field of device centralized control.

#### Q: What are the core improvements of Pivot⁺ over Pivot in terms of functions and capabilities?

A: Focusing on users' core needs in device management, Pivot⁺ has achieved comprehensive improvements in four key areas, making it more user-friendly and efficient:
Significantly Improved Enrollment Efficiency
Batch import device serial numbers, MAC addresses, and grouping information in advance. Once the device is powered on and connected to the network, it will automatically complete enrollment—no need for on-site manual binding.
The system will automatically scan all MAXHUB devices connected to the same local area network and list basic device information. Administrators only need to check the required devices to quickly complete batch binding, saving time and effort.
Notably Optimized Management Efficiency
Intuitive visual charts and calendar views clearly display real-time device status, pending tasks, and Anomaly information. This helps administrators break free from complicated data and quickly focus on key points.
Supports up to 8-level grouping management, enabling device control by spatial dimensions. It is easily adaptable to large multinational enterprises and chain retail scenarios, allowing for more refined management.
Fully Upgraded O&M Efficiency
Supports simultaneous remote control of multiple devices in the same meeting room. When controlling one device, you can synchronously view the response status of other devices, making troubleshooting and collaborative O&M more efficient—no need to rush between different locations.
Firmware upgrades support both main devices and peripherals. You can upload firmware packages independently for batch updates, or the system will automatically detect firmware versions and intelligently upgrade to the latest, ensuring devices always operate at their best.
Custom hierarchical device alerting and full-dimensional log retention ensure fast response to Anomalyities. O&M processes are safe, controllable, compliant, and traceable.
More Flexible and Convenient Content Distribution
Freely manage various resource types (images, videos, audio, documents, canvases, etc.). The system automatically categorizes and archives resources for easy search and management.
Enjoy flexible layouts when creating promotional content. You can freely arrange various components and multimedia resources to meet promotional needs in diverse scenarios, such as in-store promotions or corporate publicity.
Supports arranging content playlists in view mode and selecting release times via drag-and-drop, making content publishing more convenient and the operation process smoother.

# Control Functions

Q: What practical efficiency improvements can Pivot⁺ bring to large-scale device deployment and O&M?
A: Addressing the pain points of large-scale device management, Pivot⁺ brings tangible efficiency improvements in two key areas—deployment and O&M—making management easier:
Deployment: Offers zero-touch enrollment services, including pre-renrollment by importing SN⁺MAC in advance and automatic discovery enrollment for newly purchased devices. This greatly reduces manual operations, significantly improves deployment efficiency, and eliminates the need for individual configuration.
O&M: Provides batch remote screen control for multiple devices in the same room. When a device causes an Anomaly incidents, it will send automatic notifications and support remote troubleshooting. Additionally, a task-oriented workbench clearly lists risks and pending tasks, making O&M simpler and more efficient—no need to travel around.
Q: Is Pivot⁺ more complex to operate and manage? Is it easy for ordinary administrators to get started?
A: No need to worry at all! Pivot⁺ optimizes the interactive experience with the core concept of "humanization and high efficiency," making it even easier to get started than traditional platforms—ordinary administrators can master it quickly:
Extremely low interaction threshold: The interface is redesigned following mainstream design standards from Google and Microsoft, adopting a "core functions on homepage, shortest operation path" design. Key entrances (such as device status checks and alert handling) require no multi-level navigation. With red, and blue indicators for device status, information is intuitive and easy to understand—no need to struggle to find what you need.
Efficient operation process: The operation path for core management actions (such as batch upgrades and device searches) is shortened by 50%. It supports multi-level filtering to locate devices and issues, eliminating the need to remember complex operation logic—you can get started right away.
Controllable learning cost: New users can independently complete core tasks such as device enrollment, status monitoring, and routine O&M after just 15 minutes of basic training. No professional IT background is required—you can easily handle the management work.

#### Q: Does Pivot⁺ support the management and monitoring of third-party devices?

A: Currently, Pivot⁺ only provides management services for MAXHUB ecosystem hardware and does not support third-party devices. If you have needs related to third-party device management, please feel free to provide feedback. We will focus on and evaluate it in subsequent product iterations!
Q: Will Pivot⁺ replace Microsoft Teams Admin Center (TAC) or Microsoft Teams Rooms Pro Management Portal?
A: Microsoft's management functions include Teams app management (settings, updates, meeting settings), Teams Rooms digital signage, and basic management of meeting room devices. We usually recommend using Pivot⁺ Pro together with Microsoft Teams Rooms Pro Management. The two can complement each other to bring you a more comprehensive and efficient management experience.

#### Q: What unique management and monitoring capabilities does Pivot⁺ offer that Microsoft Teams Admin Center (TAC) or Microsoft Teams Rooms Pro Management Portal do not have?

A: Microsoft's management capabilities for Android devices are not as robust as those for Windows devices. Pivot⁺ provides more professional Android device management tools, such as remote desktop control for Android meeting room devices, which can help you solve problems more quickly. In addition, Pivot⁺ also has basic CMS service capabilities, including canvas resource creation, playlist arrangement, and schedule management, bringing you a more convenient content distribution experience for daily management.
