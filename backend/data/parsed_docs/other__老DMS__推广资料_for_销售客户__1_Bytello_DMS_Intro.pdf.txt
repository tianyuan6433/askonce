DMS (Device Management System)
DMS is a web-based software simpliﬁes the management of your campus equipment. With the DMS software, you can enjoy cluster management functionality for your interactive ﬂat panels. Options include status monitoring, panel grouping, time and channel switching, volume control, and information bulletins.
