4.2.3 WHAT LICENSE QUANTITY TO INPUT WHEN CREATING? Licenses quantity depends on how many devices are in this organization and how many years they want to manage.  1 DMS license allows one enrolled device to use 365 days(1 year) Example:  Five devices to use for one year; the organization needs ﬁve licenses.  Ten devices to use for two years; the organization needs 20 licenses.  Twenty devices to use for three years; the organization needs 60 licenses. How have DMS licenses been consumed? Licenses will convert to credits after being assigned to an organization. 1 DMS license =  365 credits One enrolled device consumes one credit one day. 4.2.4 ADD LICENSES TO AN EXISTING ORGANIZATION Supports adding licenses for organizations that are in [In use], [Expired], and [Insuﬃcient]. How to add licenses to an organization Step 1: DMS product > Locate user >  (Hover) [In use], [Expired], or [Insuﬃcient] state -> [Add licenses] 
Step 2: Input license quantity

What's the "Period of use”? Option 1 - Select a new period of use (1/2/3/5 years) Expiration date = Original expiration date + 1/2/3/5 years Note: Remaining credits in the organization will expire on the new expiration date. Option 2 - Select Keep the original expiration date Expiration date = Original expiration date Note: Total number of licenses is determined by the number of devices, 1 device = 1 license.

4.3 DISTRIBUTE LICENSES 4.3.1 WHAT IS ‘DISTRIBUTION RIGHT’ Users with ‘Distribution right' in DMS product means they can: 
Invite a new user to become their sub-account  > Chapter 2 
Open DMS free trial for their sub-account > Chapter 4.1 
Create DMS organization for their sub-account > Chapter 4.2 
Grant ‘Distribution right’ to their sub-account to let them self-manage >Chapter 4.3.2 4.3.2 GRANT ‘DISTRIBUTION RIGHT’ ONLY Step 1: Manager > License Management > (Hover) No distribution authorization state > [Authorize] Note: Only support to Grant ‘Distribution right’ to sub-accounts in [No distribution authorization] state. 
Step 2: [Authorize] 
Step 3: If you need to distribute licenses to a sub-account, click the [Distribute license now]. On the contrary,  just click [Distribute later] or close the dialog.
