1. INTRODUCTION
1.1 What does the User Center can do?
User Center is a user & product authority management platform built for product managers and intermediaries. The main functions include authorization, licenses renewal, and sub-account management.
1.2 What license Quantity to input when creating?
Licenses quantity depends on how many devices are in this organization and how many years they want to manage.
1 Bytello Hugo DMS license allows one enrolled device to use 365 days(1 year)
Example:
Five devices to use for one year; the organization needs ﬁve licenses.
Ten devices to use for two years; the organization needs 20 licenses.
Twenty devices to use for three years; the organization needs 60 licenses.
1.3 How have Bytello Hugo DMS licenses been consumed?
Licenses will convert to credits after being assigned to an organization.
1 Bytello Hugo DMS license =  365 credits
One enrolled device consumes one credit one day.
2. ADD SUB-ACCOUNT
How to add a sub-account?
2.1 SEND INVITATION
2.1.1 Enter email
Click the button [+Sub-account], enter the E-mail, and send an invitation email with a unique registration link.
2.1.2 Register for a Sub-account
The Sub-account will receive an E-mail, he/she needs to click the button [Register] , fill the registration form and submit.
2.2 Approval
2.2.1 Notification of new application
Once your sub-account submits the registration form, you will receive a notification email.
2.2.2 Approve application
Click the button [Agree] to approve the application.
Once approved, this user becomes your sub-account and appears in the list.
After approval, the sub-account will receive an email. Click the blue button to enter uc.bytello.com to manage licenses.
GRANT 'DISTRIBUTION RIGHT'
3.1 Authorize sub-account
Step:  Manager -> License management -> [License distribution] -> [Authorize]
After authorization for your sub-account, your sub-account can invite others to become their sub-account.
3.2 Distribute sub-account
Step: Manager -> License management -> [License distribution] -> [Distribute]
After distribution, your sub-account will have some licenses from you. They can then create an organization for their sub-account and add a license to their sub-account's organization. Alternatively, they can distribute a license to their sub-account."
If you leave licenses as '0' (Zero) when granting 'Distribution right', it means the user can not create an organization because they do not have enough licenses.
SUB-ACCOUNT MANAGE
4.1 CHECK LICENSES
Parent-accounts need to assign Bytello Hugo DMS licenses when creating an organization. Make sure you have enough.
4.1.1 Check your available licenses
If you do not have enough licenses, approach your parent-account for more.
Check your parent-account
4.2 CREATE A ORGANIZATION
Step 1: Locate user -> Click [Create organization]
If you can't find the user email, you should invite a new user to become their sub-account. See chapter 2
Step 2: Input licenses quantity
After the license distribution is completed, the user will receive an email. Click the blue button to activate the organization to enter hugo.bytello.com for device management.
4.3 ADD LICENSES TO AN EXISTING ORGANIZATION
Step 1: Locate user -> Bytello Hugo product -> [Add licenses]
Step 2: Input license quantity
Example:
1 device * 1year = 1 licenses have been added to the organization, converted to 1* 365 = 365 credits
Existing 1095 credits + 365 credits = 1460 credits
